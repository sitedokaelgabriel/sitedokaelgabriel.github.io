    About

    “If I have seen further than others, it is by standing upon the shoulders of giants.” (Isaac Newton)”

    I am a Mechanical Engineer, with MsC in Ocean Engineering, actually doing research in Computer Science in University of Campinas (Unicamp) under the guidance of Esther Colombini. We work on Robotics, Artificial Intelligence with a focus on Deep Reinforcement Learning. Although I have a background on computer simulation for heat transfer, Constructal Theory and Fluid Mechanics, I’ve decided to change subjects. Currently I am on the LSC - (Lab of Computer Systems).

    Now I am particularly interested in improving our understanding of Deep Learning applied to Rienforcement Learning, especially in Robotics and Imitation Learning. Not only I like to be on top of cutting edge research, but I also like to use my knowledge to help bussiness to solve real problems.

    I strongly believe in open science and reproducible research, as well as a open and well documented code on Github.

    Other interests are Politics, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Classical Music, Entrepeneurship, Innovation and Science.
